The Best Movie According to Kamil

My top 3 animes of all the time

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime about Monkey D. Luffy, a boy with rubber powers who dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates. He gathers a crew and sails the seas in search of the legendary treasure called One Piece. The series is known for its adventure, friendship, and epic battles.

The Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a historical anime based on the Viking era. It follows Thorfinn, a young warrior seeking revenge against the man who killed his father. As he grows, the story explores themes of violence, revenge, and redemption. Set in medieval Europe, it blends intense action with deep emotional and philosophical struggles.
